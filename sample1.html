Estah


People | Planet | Partnerships


Estah is a purpose-driven, pro-farmer patron devoted to championing the cause of small and marginal rural farmers who depend on agriculture for their livelihoods.


The goal is to help farmers increase productivity and rofits, add value to perishables and minimize wastage achieve sustainable economic growth and lead a better quality of life through a range of spirited interventions including: > Counseling > Knowledge-sharing > Training > GIVE’ing


With a broader focus on People, Planet, and Partnerships, Estah strives to foster economic development through a circular economy to enable measurable benefits for agriculture, society, and the environment



Estah has joined forces with 14 like-minded influencers, investors, and institutions across disciplines to help achieve its mission and has already touched the lives of 4.7 million rural youth and farmers in the states of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu so far.



SVR


Group United


SVR Group United is an entrepreneurial-accelerator focused on helping startups build transformational businesses that not only disrupt markets and generate wealth, but also drive job creation, help in nation building, and actively give back to society. While the group is focused on early stage investment opportunities, it also engages in late stage investments and buyout transactions depending on the potential.


SVR works with ambitious, astute entrepreneurs in these priority sectors including Technology, Clean Energy, Infrastructure, Retail, Travel & Hospitality, Media, Logistics, Retail/e-commerce, Waste Management, and more.


SVR Group United also devotes considerable time, efforts, and resources to a range of CSR initiatives. While Education and healthcare, are some of the priorities, SVR has also been doing its bit in the fields of environment conservation and organic farming; the group has been associated with Estah for a considerable period and has been supporting all the farmer, and environment-related initiatives.


SVR Group United is the key Sponsor for “Women on Run” and is playing an instrumental role in ensuring the success of this far-reaching initiative.
